Profil PT Antam Tbk.

ANTAM, member of MIND ID (Mining Industry Indonesia), the SOE's Holding Mining Industry is a vertically integrated, export-oriented, diversified mining and metals company. With operations spread throughout the mineral-rich Indonesian archipelago, ANTAM undertakes all activities from exploration, excavation, processing through to marketing of nickel ore, ferronickel, gold, silver, bauxite and coal. The company has long term loyal blue chip customers in Europe and Asia. Due to the vastness of the company's licensed exploration areas as well as its known large holdings of high quality reserves and resources, ANTAM has formed several joint ventures with international partners to profitably develop geological ore bodies into profitable mines.

Segmen usaha emas dan pemurnian ANTAM terdiri dari komoditas emas, jasa pengolahan dan pemurnian emas, perak dan logam mulia.

Bauksit merupakan sumber utama produksi bijih alumunium.

Segmen operasi nikel ANTAM terdiri dari penjualan feronikel dan bijih nikel.

Unit Geomin adalah salah satu dari lima unit operasi PT ANTAM Tbk (“ANTAM”).
